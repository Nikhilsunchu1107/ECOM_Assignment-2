CRM Strategy

How EstateIQ manages leads, nurtures client relationships, and drives conversions through a structured Customer Relationship Management system.

Strategy

Our CRM Approach

🎯

Lead Scoring & Segmentation

Every lead is automatically scored based on budget, intent signals, engagement frequency, and property type preference. High-score leads are prioritized for immediate follow-up.

🔔

Automated Nurture Sequences

Leads who don't convert immediately are enrolled in drip email/WhatsApp sequences — property alerts, market updates, and agent check-ins — to keep EstateIQ top-of-mind.

📊

Agent Performance Tracking

Each agent's response time, conversion rate, client feedback, and deal pipeline is tracked. Data-driven coaching improves agent quality and customer satisfaction.

🔄

Post-Sale Retention

After a transaction, clients receive post-move support, home loan reminders, rental management tips, and re-engagement campaigns when it's time to upgrade or invest again.

📱

Omnichannel Communication

CRM integrates email, WhatsApp, phone calls, and in-app notifications into a single view per client. No lead falls through the cracks, no message goes unanswered.

🤖

AI-Powered Recommendations

Machine learning analyzes browsing behavior and search patterns to suggest the right properties to the right buyer at the right time — increasing engagement and conversion.
