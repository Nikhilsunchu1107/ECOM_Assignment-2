How EstateIQ generates sustainable revenue across multiple streams while delivering value to buyers, sellers, and agents.

Overview

Multiple Revenue Streams

EstateIQ operates on a diversified model — combining subscription plans, transaction commissions, advertising, and value-added services to ensure stable and scalable income.

📋
Subscription Plans
Agents and builders pay monthly or annual fees to list properties, access leads, and use advanced CRM tools on our platform.
₹999 – ₹4,999 / month
💰
Transaction Commission
A 0.5% – 2% commission is charged on successful property transactions facilitated through EstateIQ's platform.
0.5% – 2% of deal value
⭐
Featured Listings
Sellers and agents can pay to boost their listing to the top of search results and get a "Featured" badge for higher visibility.
₹2,000 – ₹15,000 / listing
📢
Display Advertising
Banks, home loan companies, interior designers, and moving services advertise on the platform to reach our high-intent audience.
CPM / CPC based model
📊
Market Intelligence Reports
Premium market trend reports, price analytics, and investment insights sold to developers, investors, and financial institutions.
₹5,000 – ₹50,000 / report
🏠
Value-Added Services
Home loan referrals (referral fee), legal documentation assistance, interior design packages, and packers & movers partnerships.
₹1,500 – ₹10,000 / service

Commission Structure

Transaction Commission Model

🏠

Residential Sale

1%
A 1% commission on the final transaction value of any residential property sale facilitated through the platform. Split 50/50 between buyer's and seller's agent.
🏢

Commercial Property

2%
Higher commission on commercial deals given greater complexity, longer sales cycles, and higher deal values. Includes dedicated agent support.
🔑

Rental Agreements

0.5%
Half-month rent equivalent charged as one-time fee when a rental deal is closed via the platform. Paid by the landlord or property manager.
🏗️

New Projects (Builders)

1.5%
Builders pay a 1.5% referral commission for every unit sold via EstateIQ, plus a flat onboarding fee to list new launch projects on the platform.
