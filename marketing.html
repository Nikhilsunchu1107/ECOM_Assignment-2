Channels

Marketing Channels in Detail

🔍

SEO & Content Marketing

Target high-intent keywords like "3 BHK flats in Mumbai", "buy apartment Bangalore under 1 crore". Publish area guides, investment tips, market reports to drive organic traffic and establish authority.

Long-tail keywords Area guides Blog content Backlink building Local SEO
📱

Social Media Marketing

Instagram & Facebook campaigns featuring property tours, success stories, and market tips. YouTube channel with neighbourhood walkthroughs. LinkedIn for B2B targeting of agents and builders.

Instagram Reels Facebook Ads YouTube Tours LinkedIn B2B
💰

Performance Marketing (PPC)

Google Search Ads targeting buyers at decision stage. Retargeting campaigns for users who viewed listings but didn't enquire. Display ads on real estate news portals and financial websites.

Google Search Ads Retargeting Display Network A/B Testing
🤝

Influencer & PR Marketing

Partner with finance influencers (Fintech YouTube channels, Instagram personal finance creators) and local lifestyle bloggers in each city. Press coverage in Economic Times Property, Mint, Moneycontrol.

Finance influencers Press releases City lifestyle blogs Podcast appearances
📧

Email & WhatsApp Marketing

Weekly "New Listings in Your Area" emails. Price drop alerts. Monthly market reports. WhatsApp Business API for instant property alerts and agent communication.

Drip campaigns Price alerts WhatsApp Business Segmented lists
🏙️

Offline & Partnership Marketing

Tie-ups with housing loan banks (HDFC, SBI Home Loans), co-branded campaigns. Property expos in Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore. Corporate tie-ups for employee housing programs.

Bank partnerships Property expos Corporate deals Builder tie-ups
